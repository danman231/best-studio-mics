What mic does Dave Grohl use?

This microphone is renowned for its versatility and warm, clear sound, making it a favorite among vocalists and producers in the music industry.

What microphone did Fleetwood Mac use?

Fleetwood Mac, like many artists of their era, used a variety of microphones throughout their recording and performing career. What mic did Freddie Mercury use?

What mic does Billie Eilish use to record?
